Our Story

A small kitchen with big intentions.

We're not a chain, we're not a restaurant. We're a couple of food-loving folks cooking out of our small home in Rupena Agrahara, attached to Silk Board & PROM.

Home  /  About

Why "Desi Monk"?

The monk reminds us that good food, like a good life, doesn't need much — just patience, fresh ingredients, and the right amount of fire. We wear the saffron-yellow apron because that's the colour of warmth, sunshine and home-cooked dal-chawal.

Bangalore has thousands of cloud kitchens — most of them mass-produce food in dark warehouses. We don't. We cook in the same kitchen our family eats from. If we wouldn't eat it, we wouldn't pack it for you.

Inside our kitchen

This is where your food is cooked.

No mass-production warehouse. No dark cloud-kitchen unit with twenty different cuisines. Just one tidy home kitchen with a gas stove, a heavy iron kadhai, hand-labelled spice jars on a small shelf, and a window that opens onto the morning sun.

Every dish you order leaves this stove, made by us. That's the whole pitch.

What we believe

Four rules our kitchen runs on

🥘

Fresh, never frozen

Chicken & mutton are picked up the same morning from a trusted local butcher. Curries simmer the moment you order.

🌿

Hand-ground masalas

We grind our own garam masala, kadai masala & chicken masala every week. No factory blends.

⚖️

Honest portions

Quarter, half, full — what you see is what you get. No "boneless premium" upcharges, no hidden taxes.

🛡️

Hygiene first

FSSAI licensed. Daily kitchen sanitisation. Sealed, leak-proof packaging. Gloves & hairnets, always.

From kitchen to door

How your order actually happens

1

You order

Direct via website / WhatsApp, or through Zomato / Swiggy. Mention spice level & any allergies.

2

We start cooking

Curries simmer for 25–40 min, mutton for 90 min. Chapatis are rolled fresh, never pre-cooked.

3

Sealed & packed

Hot food in tamper-evident, leak-proof boxes with onion-lemon, salt sachets & tissue.

4

Delivered warm

Within 3 km — our own rider, free. Beyond — Zomato/Swiggy. 35–45 min average.

Compliance
