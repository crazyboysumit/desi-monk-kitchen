Ordering

How do I place an order?
Three quick options: WhatsApp (fastest), call, or fill the form on our order page. We confirm in < 5 min.
Do you have a minimum order?
Yes — ₹150 for free delivery within 3 km. No minimum on Zomato / Swiggy (their fees apply).
Can I schedule the order for later?
Absolutely. Mention your preferred time — we cook to that slot, not before, so it arrives hot.
Can I customise spice level?
Yes — say mild, medium or spicy. We can also do "extra masala" or "low oil" on request.

Delivery

What's your delivery zone?
Free within 3 km from Rupena Agrahara — Silk Board, BTM, Bommanahalli, Viratnagar, HSR Sector 7, Madiwala, Hongasandra. See delivery page for full list.
How long does delivery take?
35–45 minutes average from confirmation. Mutton dishes need ~60 min as they're slow-cooked. Sunday lunch peak may add 10–15 min.
I live more than 3 km away — can you still deliver?
Yes, on Zomato or Swiggy (up to ~10 km). For 3–5 km direct delivery, call us — a small fee may apply.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
Cash on delivery and UPI for direct orders. Cards/wallets/UPI on Zomato/Swiggy.
Is the 50% off discount permanent?
It's a launch offer, applicable on direct orders. We'll announce when it changes — early customers will get loyalty perks.

Food & Allergens

Is your food really home-style?
Yes — we cook in a small home kitchen, the same place our family eats from. No commercial griddles, no factory-made gravies.
Do you handle allergens?
We use dairy, wheat, eggs, mustard, nuts & ghee in the kitchen. We can avoid specific items on request, but cannot guarantee a fully allergen-free environment.
Are the chickens / mutton fresh?
Yes — picked fresh each morning from a trusted local butcher. Never frozen, never re-frozen.
Do you serve vegetarian food?
Yes — chapati, jeera rice, dal tadka, raita, papad & salad. We're working on adding more veg curries based on demand.

Hygiene & Compliance

Are you FSSAI licensed?
Yes — we are FSSAI registered. License number will be displayed on the website & on packaging soon.
How is the food packed?
Tamper-evident, leak-proof, food-grade containers. Sealed before dispatch. Cold packs & insulation for longer routes.

Refunds & Issues

What if I get the wrong order?
Full refund or replacement, your choice. WhatsApp us within 30 minutes of delivery.
Can I cancel after ordering?
Free until cooking starts. After that a 40% charge applies. See full refund policy.

Catering & Bulk

Do you do bulk / party orders?
Yes — for 10+ people, please order at least 24 hours in advance. Email hello@desimonkkitchen.com.
Office tiffin / monthly subscription?
In planning. WhatsApp us & we'll add you to the early-access list.
