Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026. Plain English — what data we collect, how we use it, and how you stay in control.

Home  /  Privacy

1. Who we are

Desi Monk Kitchen ("we", "us") operates this website and a small home cloud kitchen at Roopena Agrahara, Bengaluru — 560068. For privacy-related queries, write to privacy@desimonkkitchen.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Order details: name, phone number, delivery address, email (optional), items ordered, special instructions.
  • Payment proof: UPI transaction ID or screenshot if shared. We do not store card data.
  • Communication: chat history on WhatsApp, email replies, voice notes from phone.
  • Website analytics: anonymised page-views, device type, broad geographic location, referring source — to understand how the site is used.

We do not collect sensitive personal data (Aadhaar, biometric, financial account details) on this website.

3. Why we collect it

  • To prepare and deliver your order accurately.
  • To contact you about order status, delays, or refunds.
  • To occasionally tell you about new menu items or offers — only if you opt in.
  • To meet legal & regulatory obligations (FSSAI, tax records).

4. How long we keep it

Order details are retained for 12 months for accounting and complaint resolution. WhatsApp chats are retained as long as our WhatsApp Business account exists. You can ask us to delete your data at any time (see "Your rights" below).

5. Sharing your data

We share your information only with:

  • Our delivery rider — name, phone, address, instructions.
  • Online platforms (Zomato, Swiggy) — when you order through them, they collect & share with us as per their privacy policies.
  • Payment processors — when applicable, for UPI / digital payments.
  • Authorities — if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights.

We never sell your personal data to advertisers, marketers, or any third party.

6. Cookies & analytics

We may use a minimal analytics tool (such as Google Analytics or Plausible) and small cookies to understand site usage. You can clear or block cookies via your browser; the website will keep working.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information — password-protected devices, encrypted communication, restricted access. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we treat your data with care.

8. Your rights

  • Ask what data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete it
  • Withdraw consent for marketing messages
  • Lodge a complaint with us or with a competent data-protection authority in India

Email privacy@desimonkkitchen.com with your request — we'll respond within 7 business days.

9. Children

Our services are not directed at children under 18. We don't knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Updates

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top and, for significant changes, post a notice on the homepage.
