Refund & Cancellation

Food is perishable, but fairness shouldn't be. Here's exactly how cancellations and refunds work.

Home  /  Refund

1. Cancellation window

  • Free cancellation — until we confirm the order and put it on the stove (usually 5–10 min after you place it).
  • Once cooking starts — you may cancel, but a 40% charge applies (we've already used the ingredients).
  • Once dispatched — order cannot be cancelled. If genuinely undeliverable, see "refund situations" below.

2. Refund situations

We offer a full or partial refund in the following cases:

  • Wrong item delivered — full refund or replacement, your choice.
  • Quality issue (food spoilt, badly under-cooked, hair / foreign object) — full refund + replacement, with a photo of the issue shared on WhatsApp within 30 minutes of delivery.
  • Missing item — refund for the missing item, or send the missing item if you're available.
  • Significantly delayed delivery (over 90 minutes from confirmation, our fault) — 25% refund + apology.
  • Allergy not addressed despite being declared — full refund + sincere apology.

3. Non-refundable cases

  • Subjective taste preferences ("too spicy", "too oily") — we'll happily accept feedback but cannot refund. Mention spice level when ordering.
  • Customer not available at delivery address; multiple call attempts ignored.
  • Wrong / incomplete address provided.
  • Damage caused after delivery (spilled, dropped, etc.).

4. How to request a refund

  • WhatsApp us at +91 80028 54477 within 30 minutes of delivery, with order details and photo evidence (if applicable).
  • We'll respond within 2 hours during open hours.
  • Approved refunds are credited within 3–5 business days to your original payment source (UPI / cash / online wallet).

5. Orders via Zomato or Swiggy

Refunds for orders placed on Zomato or Swiggy are handled directly by those platforms as per their respective policies. We coordinate with them on quality issues but cannot directly process the refund.

6. Final word

We're a small kitchen run by people, not a faceless chain. If there's a genuine issue, just tell us. We'll make it right — sometimes more than what the policy strictly requires, because we'd rather you come back than win an argument.
